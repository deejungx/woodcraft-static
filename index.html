What We Do To Make The World A Better Place

Woodcraft Gallery is the heart of Arts and Crafts for Makers, Lovers and all Art enthusiasts. It is an initiative from the Bajracharya family: a family nationally renowned for preserving and promoting the Traditional Arts and Crafts of Nepal. We aim to provide affordable and trustworthy transit point for future generations of Nepalese Artists towards achieving eminence and prosperity on a Global scale.
